Make Apple Potion!

You must prepare a magical potion by catching apples using the movement of the cauldron (keys "A" and "D"). To win, score 100 points, but the more points you earn, the better your potion will be.

Magic of Apples:

Red apples are good! For each red apple you catch, you will earn 1 point.

Green apples explode when they fall! If you fail to catch a green apple, it will explode, emitting toxic fumes, and you will lose one life. Don't forget about this, otherwise you may lose IF YOU DON'T CATCH three green apples before reaching 100 points.

Golden apples are the rarest. If you catch a golden apple, you will earn a whopping 15 points!
